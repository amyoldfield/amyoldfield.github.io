Our Services

The Cheshire Mobile Crèche is a privately owned company providing bespoke, professional crèche services for weddings and other one-off events in the North West of England.
We work with you to create the perfect package for your special day.
Our friendly team of experienced childcare professionals transform a suitable space at your venue into a safe and fun child-friendly area.
Just tell us how many children you would like us to care for, the amount of time you require the crèche facility and any special requests. We will then design a package that can be adapted to your individual needs.

  • Premium
  • Let our qualified, experienced staff entertain your children in a separate room at your venue.
    We transform a room into a fun and safe environment that will keep your little guests entertained for hours.
    As each crèche is bespoke we can focus on themes and activities that suit the age range and interests of the children attending.
  • In Room
  • This package is provided in the same room as the event and is ideal for venues that don’t have additional rooms to host a full crèche service. Quiet play and activities can be provided enabling parents to enjoy the event without interruption.
  • Evening
  • We offer an into the evening service up until 10pm. Children will be given the opportunity to relax with dvd’s and story time, giving them the opportunity to wind down after the activities of the day.
  • This service can be booked in addition to our premium crèche service.

Some examples of activities we could incorporate into your crèche

  • Traditional party games - musical bumps, pass the parcel, silly statues
  • Arts and crafts - dècoupage, colouring, playdoh, easel board
  • Traditional outdoor games - croquèt, sack race, egg and spoon race
  • Face painting and glitter tattoos
  • Karaoke, Zumba kids, rhythm and rhyme with musical instruments
  • Indoor games - parachute play, ride on toys, tents and tunnels

We are always open to suggestions, the list is endless!

Our Standards

With children of our own we understand the importance of quality childcare.

We hold a database of carefully selected childcare practitioners to enable us to provide the correct staff coverage.

Our team are DBS checked and have experience of working with children. All members of staff wear a uniform so parents and children can recognise us easily.

The Cheshire Mobile Crèche strictly adhere to the following Ofsted staff to child ratios.

Age range Staff : Children
0 - 2 years 1 : 3
2 - 3 years 1 : 4
3+ years 1 : 8

e.g. for 6 children under 2 years old, we would have 2 staff members dedicated to their care.
